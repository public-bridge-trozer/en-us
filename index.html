What is Trézór Bridge?

    Trézór Bridge is a small application that allows your computer to communicate with your Trézór hardware wallet. Without it, your device won’t be able to connect to the setup interface at Trézór.io/start or work with wallet software on your computer. It's a vital part of the system that makes everything run smoothly behind the scenes.

    Once you install Trézór Bridge, it stays active in the background, quietly managing the connection between your Trézór device and your browser or app. This secure connection allows you to send, receive, and manage your crypto with confidence.
